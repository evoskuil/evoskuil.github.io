VERSIONS

Cryptoeconomics evolved over a couple of years as a series of topics on the libbitcoin-system GitHub wiki. The 1st book edition was printed in Hanoi for CryptoEcon 2020 in February 2020, and contains minor changes from the early wiki. The wiki is maintained and in sync with current editions, but contains no images. The 2nd edition was released globally on Amazon as a paperback and soon after as a Kindle e-book. There are no changes to the list of chapters between the two editions, though there are some material content changes. The 2nd edition is now also available as a free pdf. Each format carries its own ISBN and a version number. The current version is 1.2.3.

BUY IT

Cryptoeconomics is available for purchase only through Amazon, as paperback and Kindle e-book.
